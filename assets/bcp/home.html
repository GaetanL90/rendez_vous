Welcome to our site!

This platform is for those patients who needs to set or request an appointment with doctor without leaving a house.

Patients will be able to apply for an appointment with desired doctor from the list and choose date they want to have an appointment on then apply!

After this doctors will receive an application containing motive/details/ reason for appointment and date, doctor will be able to approve or decline an application and provide feedback to patient in case of decline.

This platform is striking the time spent by patients and doctors trying to agree on an appointment to be reduced hence it canalso make changes to the overflow that happen to be at hospitals since that service can be issued from home.
